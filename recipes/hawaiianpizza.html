Hawaiian Pizza

A legendary flavour that makes people question it. The Hawaiian pizza was invented by a Greek living in Canada.
With the rice of sweet-and-savory dishes, he decided to try something unusual: adding canned pineapple to a pizza
along with ham.

The pineapple brand he used was called “Hawaiian,” which is how the pizza got its name!
No deeper tropical cultural connection! Despite the controversy, Hawaiian pizza spread worldwide.
Today it’s considered a classic menu item—beloved, hated, and memed forever.

Ratatouille

The jewel of French cuisine, is a dazzling ingredients of vegetables—each lovingly sautéed
to preserve its texture and flavour, then simmered together to create a rich, harmonious dish.
The result is a visual stunning succulent meal, perfectly to be eaten warm and where every
bite tells a story.

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Bell Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. Prepare the vegetables
  2. Salt the eggplant
  3. Sauté vegetables separately
  4. Cook the tomatoes and garlic
  5. Combine all vegetables
  6. Finish and serve
